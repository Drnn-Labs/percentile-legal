2. Description of Service

Percentile is a personal utility application that allows parents and caregivers to log infant growth measurements — weight, height, and head circumference — and visualize those measurements against the WHO Child Growth Standards (2006) reference curves.

Important: Percentile is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not a substitute for professional pediatric care. Growth percentile calculations are provided for informational and reference purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider regarding your child's health and development.

The App operates entirely offline. All computations, including WHO percentile calculations, are performed on your device using embedded reference data. No measurement data is transmitted to any server.

7. Medical Disclaimer

Percentile uses WHO Child Growth Standards (2006) as its reference dataset. This is the internationally recognized standard recommended by the CDC and AAP for children aged 0–24 months. The calculations performed by the App are intended to replicate these published standards accurately.

However:

  • The App does not provide diagnoses, clinical assessments, or medical advice
  • Growth percentile information should be interpreted by a qualified healthcare provider in the context of your child's full clinical picture
  • The App does not account for corrected gestational age in premature infants
  • A single measurement or percentile value is rarely clinically meaningful in isolation — trends over time are what matter clinically

If you have concerns about your child's growth or development, consult your pediatrician. Do not delay seeking professional medical advice based on information from this App.
